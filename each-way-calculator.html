How each way betting works

An each way bet is two bets: one on the selection to win and one on the selection to place. The stake you enter is the stake for each part, so the total outlay is always double that amount.

What place terms mean

Place terms decide the odds paid on the place part of the bet. At 8/1 with 1/4 odds, the place part pays at 2/1. If the selection wins, both the win and place parts return. If it places without winning, only the place part returns.

Common each way examples

  • €10 each way at 8/1 and 1/4 odds costs €20 total.
  • If it wins, the win part returns €90 and the place part returns €30, for €120 total.
  • If it places without winning, only the place part returns €30, which is €10 profit after the €20 total stake.
